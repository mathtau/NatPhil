Joining Steps

Add = join steps along the line.   2 + 3 = 5
Order is free (commute):   2 + 3 = 3 + 2
Regroup freely (associate):   (1+2) = 3, so (1+2)+3 = 1+(2+3) = 6
…yet some whisper 1 + 1 = 3. 🤔 A riddle for a land far ahead.
